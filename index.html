Organization Brief

NEW AFRICAN MANDATE(NAM) is a league of young people working to find solutions to poverty, injustice and poor governance in Africa with the purpose to catalyze the emergence of a new Africa with better opportunities for all in the continent. We lead community-based efforts to improve basic education, expand economic opportunities, agriculture, gender justice, health, peace, good governance and protect the environment. Through advocacy, campaigning, policy research and development projects, N.A.M remains committed to making remarkable difference and touching the lives of many.
